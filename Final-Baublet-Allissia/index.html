About Me

Who I Am

I'm a small town girl who grew up caring for farm animals, challenging myself with video games, educating myself through literature, and expressing myself through art. I have continued the last three activities as hobbies in my spare time with the addition of research related to my studies. I love to spend time with my friends and family playing games and cooking together.

Where I'm From

My home is in Wells County, Indiana, a small town called Poneto. It's surrounded by cornfields and known to the locals (at least the teenagers) as snoozeville. However, I enjoy sitting on the porch, in the peace and quiet, and reading or drawing. With Poneto being such a small town, moving to Bloomington for college was terrifying and eye-opening all at once.

What I Aspire to Be

I never had an interest in technology, except for videogames, as a child. We didn't have wifi in my home, and my school wasn't up to date in terms of equipment or courses. My first experience with coding was developing a website during my freshman year of college, and I absolutely fell in love with it. The creativity and challenge of making a site from scratch drew me in like... a cat to a ball of yarn. Since then, my goal in life has been to become a Web Developer.
